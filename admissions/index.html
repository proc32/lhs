Broncos are ambitious problem-solvers. They care about the world around them. SCU gives them the knowledge, experiences, and opportunities to make it better. Are you one of us?

An Education That Gives You More Than a Degree

From psychology to sustainable-energy engineering, theatre arts to business analytics, Santa Clara’s undergraduate, graduate, and international programs transform the way students see the world and their place in it.

 

Undergraduate Admission

With more than 50 majors, minors, and special programs to choose from, an SCU education is built around putting theory to practice and taking a holistic approach to preparing you for your future.

 

Graduate Admission

Whether you’re setting out on a course of academic study that will define your professional life, or seeking to build skills to help you land a better job, SCU's graduate programs offer a range of options to match your needs. 

It's Good to be a Bronco
Being in the heart of Silicon Valley means you’re down the street from some of the most innovative and successful businesses in the world. An SCU education is an investment that will pay off throughout the course of your life, both financially and personally.
