Why Choose LHS

Students of all backgrounds flourish here. One thing they have in common? They want to make a difference. Leading High School students are driven to build a better, kinder, more humane, more sustainable planet. And everything they need is right here. With our location, dedicated community of faculty, and opportunities inside and outside the classroom, we teach you to think critically, take risks, and take charge.
